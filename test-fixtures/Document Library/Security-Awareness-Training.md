# Security Awareness Training Program

## Purpose

Outlines the organization's security awareness training requirements and curriculum.

## Training Requirements

| Role | Frequency | Duration | Topics |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|
| All employees | Annual | 2 hours | General security |
| Developers | Semi-annual | 4 hours | Secure coding |
| Managers | Annual | 3 hours | Risk management |
| Executives | Annual | 1 hour | Strategic risk |

## Core Curriculum

### Module 1: Phishing Awareness
- Identifying suspicious emails
- Reporting procedures
- Social engineering tactics

### Module 2: Data Protection
- Classification handling
- Encryption requirements
- Clean desk policy

### Module 3: Physical Security
- Badge access protocols
- Visitor management
- Secure area procedures

### Module 4: Incident Reporting
- What to report
- How to report
- Escalation paths
