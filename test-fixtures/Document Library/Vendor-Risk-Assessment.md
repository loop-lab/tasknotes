# Vendor Risk Assessment Framework

## Purpose

Defines how the organization evaluates and manages risks from third-party vendors and service providers.

## Risk Categories

| Category | Weight | Examples |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Data access | 30% | Handles PII, financial data |
| System integration | 25% | API access, network connectivity |
| Business continuity | 20% | Single point of failure |
| Compliance | 15% | Regulatory requirements |
| Reputation | 10% | Public-facing services |

## Assessment Process

### Tier 1 - Critical Vendors
- Full on-site assessment
- Annual SOC 2 report review
- Quarterly risk scoring
- Contractual SLA monitoring

### Tier 2 - Important Vendors
- Remote questionnaire assessment
- Annual compliance verification
- Semi-annual risk scoring

### Tier 3 - Standard Vendors
- Self-assessment questionnaire
- Annual review

## Scoring Matrix

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 90-100 | Low risk | Continue monitoring |
| 70-89 | Medium risk | Remediation plan required |
| 50-69 | High risk | Escalate to management |
| < 50 | Critical risk | Suspend engagement |
